Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra Review

Samsung's 2023 powerhouse - Does it beat iPhone 15 Pro?

Samsung Galaxy S23 Ultra

★★★★★ (4.8/5)

The S23 Ultra features a 200MP camera, built-in S Pen, and Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 chip. The ultimate Android alternative to iPhone.

Quick Specs:

  • • Display: 6.8" Dynamic AMOLED 2X (120Hz)
  • • Chipset: Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 (4nm)
  • • Camera: 200MP + 12MP + 10MP + 10MP
  • • Battery: 5,000mAh (45W fast charging)
  • • Special: Built-in S Pen
  • • Price in PKR: Rs. 350,000

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Best smartphone display (1750 nits brightness)

Cons

Detailed Review

Design & S Pen Features

The S23 Ultra maintains Samsung's contoured-cut camera design with more refined edges. The built-in S Pen transforms this into a productivity powerhouse.

"The S Pen latency is now just 2.8ms - feels like writing on real paper with Air Command features"

Available in 4 colors: Phantom Black, Cream, Green, Lavender.

200MP Camera System

The 200MP main sensor uses pixel binning for stunning 12.5MP shots with incredible detail. New Astro Hyperlapse and enhanced Nightography.

Key camera features:

  • 200MP high-resolution mode
  • 10x optical zoom (100x Space Zoom)
  • Expert RAW mode for professionals
  • 8K video recording

Performance & Battery Life

The Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 (custom for Samsung) delivers 40% better GPU performance than S22 Ultra. New vapor chamber cooling system.

Geekbench 6 Score: 1,900 (Single-core)

5,000mAh battery lasts up to 1.5 days with moderate use. Supports 45W fast charging (0-65% in 30 mins).

Final Verdict

9.3/10

The S23 Ultra is the most complete Android phone with best display, versatile camera system, and unique S Pen. However, its large size and slow charging might bother some users.

Best for: Android enthusiasts, photographers, business users.

Not for: Those who prefer compact phones or iOS ecosystem.
