iPhone 15 Pro Review

Apple's 2024 powerhouse - Worth Rs. 450,000 in Pakistan?

The iPhone 15 Pro features a titanium design, A17 Pro chip, and revolutionary camera system. But is it worth the premium price?

Detailed Review

Design & Build Quality

The iPhone 15 Pro introduces a titanium frame, making it the lightest Pro model ever. The matte glass back feels premium, and the new "Action Button" replaces the mute switch.

"The titanium build makes a noticeable difference in daily use - 19g lighter than 14 Pro!"

Available in 4 colors: Black Titanium, White Titanium, Blue Titanium, Natural Titanium.

Camera Performance

The 48MP main sensor now supports 24MP default photos (smart pixel binning). New 5x telephoto lens (Pro Max only) and improved Night Mode.

Key camera features:

  • 48MP ProRAW support
  • Next-gen Smart HDR
  • 4K60 ProRes video
  • Improved portrait mode

Performance & Battery

The A17 Pro chip (3nm) delivers 10% faster CPU and 20% faster GPU than A16. New 6-core GPU supports hardware-accelerated ray tracing.

Geekbench 6 Score: 2,900 (Single-core)

Battery life lasts up to 23 hours video playback (similar to 14 Pro). Supports 20W fast charging (50% in 30 mins).

Final Verdict

9.5/10

The iPhone 15 Pro is the most powerful smartphone in 2024 with best-in-class performance and camera. However, the high price in Pakistan makes it hard to recommend unless money is no object.

Best for: Professionals, content creators, Apple ecosystem users.

Not for: Budget buyers or those who don't need pro features.
